Mind Coach – Cookies Policy

Last Updated: 2025

Welcome to Mind Coach! Each time you use the Application, we use certain digital tools to personalize and improve your experience. This policy explains what these tools — cookies and similar technologies — are, what they do, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your device. They help us understand how you use the Application and allow us to offer a more personalized experience. They do not directly contain your identity; rather, they work like digital notes that remember your preferences and usage patterns.

2. What Types of Cookies Do We Use?

Mind Coach may use different types of cookies for various purposes:

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the basic operation of the Application. They support core features such as session management, security, and main screen navigation.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand which areas of the Application are used more frequently and which screens may need improvement. This enables us to continually improve our system.

Personalization Cookies

These cookies remember your personal settings such as language, theme, and preferred features. They help create an experience that recognizes you each time you open the Application.

Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to show you more relevant content and offers. Some of them may operate in connection with third-party platforms.

3. Why Do We Use Cookies?

Mind Coach uses cookies for the following purposes:

To ensure the Application runs quickly and smoothly

To remember your preferences and reduce repetitive actions

To make the experience more user-friendly and personalized

To measure which features are most used and plan improvements

To optimize relevant content and advertising

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by partners providing analytics and advertising services. These cookies are managed in accordance with the policies of the respective platforms. Mind Coach takes care to work with such partners in a way that respects data security and privacy.

5. Cookie Controls and Management

You have full control over cookies. If you wish, you can:

Restrict or block cookies via your browser settings

Manage permissions through your device settings

Control cookie usage through in-app settings (where available)

However, disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent the Application from functioning properly.

6. Use of Data

If the information obtained through cookies qualifies as personal data, such data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy. Mind Coach uses your data only within the scope of legal requirements and through secure methods.

7. Updates to This Policy

Technologies and standards may change over time. Therefore, this Cookies Policy may be updated. The most current version will always be available within the Application or on our website.
